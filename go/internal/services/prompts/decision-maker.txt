## Conflict Resolution Rules

2. If dates are available, prefer the value from the MOST RECENT source. Record all conflicting values and dates in your reasoning.
3. If no dates are available, apply these tiebreakers in order:
   a. Official/primary source (company site, government registry, SEC filing) over secondary sources
